# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive experience for everyone. We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, healthy community.

## Standards

**Positive behavior:**
- Being respectful and constructive
- Giving and accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Any conduct that could be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to [contact@novastorm.ai](mailto:contact@novastorm.ai). All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
